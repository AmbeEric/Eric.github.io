From The Chairman

The Cameroon Development Coperation (CDC)Has an issued shared capital of 53.5 billion FCFA with over 14,456 employees and it is own by the government. It was formed in 1947 for the purpose of developing and running plantations of tropical crops in the country. The CDC is an Agribusiness company and its general offices are based in Bota, Limbe. Its plantation covers a total of approximately 41,000 Hectares of land, 38,000 Hectares of which is mature and of production stage.
Hope Sona (Chairman)
